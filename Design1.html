Fun crafts

Fun craft ideas include wood burning, candle painting, making fluid art bears, and perfume making. Other engaging options are origami, friendship bracelets, and needlework. For kids, consider flower pens, unicorn crafts, or transforming wooden spoons.
Needlework is a fabulous craft for beginners as you can start with very small and simple designs. Cross stitch is a very simple craft which is created by stitching little crosses into a design. It's quick and easy to master!
Handmade jewelry are also a popular pick!
